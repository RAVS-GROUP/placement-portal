About the Placement Portal

Empowering Women, Girls & Youth Through Sustainable Careers

At Udayan Care, we believe that education and skill development become truly meaningful when they lead to dignified and sustainable livelihoods.

Our Career Development & Placement Portal connects trained beneficiaries with employers, internships, apprenticeships, and career opportunities across diverse sectors.

We work closely with industry partners to bridge the gap between talent and opportunity while supporting candidates throughout their employment journey.
